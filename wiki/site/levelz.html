LevelZ

Experience for skills is gained at the same rate as normal experience and from the same sources Experience for skills is also gained by leveling up jobs

Common Experience Examples Exp

Killing most hostile mobs 5
Killing a player 7 per level
Mining ore 1-7
Cooking/Smelting ~1
Breeding animals 1-7
Catching Fish 1-6
Trading with villagers 3-6
